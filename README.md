## 2. Problem Statement
The rapid evolution of social media platforms has transformed daily life into a continuous digital experience. While technology provides convenience and connectivity, excessive usage has led to:

- Increased anxiety and overthinking  
- Comparison culture and low self-confidence  
- Reduced face-to-face interaction  
- Sleep disorders and mental exhaustion  
- Shortened attention spans  

Digital addiction has become a silent behavioral crisis affecting productivity, relationships, and psychological stability. There is a strong need for awareness through strategic visual communication.
